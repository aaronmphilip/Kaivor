Mission

Make Android feel like a worker, not a pile of apps.

Operate the real UI

We care about actual phone state: accessibility trees, screenshots, notifications, overlays, app recovery, and proof that actions worked.

Ask before risk

An agent that submits wrong forms is worse than no agent. Missing info, sensitive steps, and irreversible actions must be explicit.

Win on speed

Users should feel the phone moving quickly. Every loop must cut latency, avoid needless vision calls, and keep progress visible.
