What it solves

Android is powerful. The busywork is the problem.

Most phone work is not hard. It is slow because it jumps across Chrome, PDFs, WhatsApp, payment apps, maps, shopping apps, and notifications. BharatClaw is built to operate that messy surface instead of only answering questions in a chat box.

How it works

Fast path first. Visual reading when the screen needs it.

BharatClaw starts with the Android accessibility tree because it is faster than screenshot-only control. It turns on visual screen reading for sparse screens, documents, form recovery, and anything the accessibility layer cannot explain.

  • Progress appears in the notch overlay and notification.
  • Gemini powers the reasoning and document understanding layer.
  • Voice notes become commands through speech-to-text.
  • Blocked tasks ask for missing information in Telegram.

Product principle

Calm interface. Relentless phone execution.

The experience should feel quiet while the work is happening: one command, visible state, exact questions when details are missing, and a final report when the Android task is done.
