FAQ

Answers for teams deciding whether to launch.

Is this only for one-person businesses?

No. BharatClaw is useful for solo owners, small teams, and growing operators who need tighter lead handling before they add more headcount.

What is live right now?

The live deployment path includes Telegram intake, owner pairing, captured leads, automated follow-ups, and a lightweight owner console.

What makes this different from a basic chatbot?

BharatClaw is designed like an operating layer, not a toy script. It stores the lead, keeps conversation state, schedules follow-ups, and preserves owner control.

BharatClaw is built to replace slow manual lead handling, not decorate it.
