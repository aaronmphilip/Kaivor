Built Right Now

What is already live in this MVP

Instant Lead Reply

When a customer opens your BharatClaw lead link on Telegram, the bot replies immediately and starts the lead flow.

Human Lead Capture

BharatClaw asks for the customer name and requirement in a natural sequence instead of robotic command prompts.

Lead Storage + Follow-up

Leads are stored in the database with timestamps, state, language preference, and follow-up scheduling flags.

Owner Control

Pair once, get owner alerts, open the owner console, view recent leads, and use manual takeover when needed.
